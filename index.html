About Me:


Welcome to Ryan Borel's Music Lesson Service! As a professional musician with 20 years of experience playing guitar, bass, piano, and ukulele, I'm excited to offer personalized music lessons to students of all ages and skill levels.


Whether you're a beginner just starting or an experienced musician looking to take your skills to the next level, I can help you achieve your musical goals. My lessons are tailored to each student's needs, so you can learn at your own pace and focus on the areas that are most important to you.


With my patient and encouraging teaching style, I strive to create a fun and engaging learning environment for my students. Whether you're interested in learning your favorite songs, mastering new techniques, or exploring different musical genres, I'm here to guide you every step of the way.


In addition to guitar, bass, piano, and ukulele lessons, I offer music theory and composition lessons for those looking to deepen their understanding of music and develop their unique style.


So whether you're a beginner or a seasoned musician, let's work together to unlock your musical potential! Contact me today to schedule your first lesson.
